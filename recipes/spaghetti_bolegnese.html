Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

Home

Description

This is the world famous spaghetti bolognese recipe made by john bolognese himself! On this page you will find all the ingredients need to make this recipe from scratch as well as the instructions to do so.

Ingredients

  • 150g spaghetti
  • 100g beef mince
  • half a small small onion (minced)
  • 1 clove of garlic (minced)
  • 200g bolegnese sauce
  • 5g of a fat of your choice
  • 10g parmesan cheese
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

  1. Heat some fat in a pan and place in your onions
  2. Once your onions are soft, place the garlic in the pan and continue cooking
  3. After 30 seconds add the beef mince and break it apart using a spatula
  4. Once the mince is browned pour in your bolegnese sauce
  5. Season immediately with: salt, and black pepper
  6. Redice the heat and let the dish simmer for 12 minutes
  7. While the bolegense is simmering, start boiling a pot of water
  8. Add some salt to the water and then add your spaghetti
  9. Once the spaghetti is done, drain it and add it to a plate
  10. Add the bolegnese on top of the spaghetti
  11. Grate some parmesan cheese on top
