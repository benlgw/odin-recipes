Cheeseburger Recipe

Description

This is the world famous cheeseburger recipe made by john cheeseburger himself! On this page you will find all the ingredients need to make this recipe from scratch as well as the instructions to do so.

Ingredients

  • 1 burger bun
  • 100g of 15% beef mince
  • 2 slices of cheddar cheese
  • 10g of butter
  • 5g of a fat of your choice
  • 10g of Tomato sauce
  • 10g of Mayonaise
  • 10g of Mustard
  • Salt
  • Black Pepper

  1. Prepare the beef mince by placing the whole 100g into a mixing bowl
  2. Add salt and pepper to taste and then mix up all the ingredients ensuring the spices get fully incorperated
  3. Once mixed let the beef sit in the fridge for at least 30 minutes
  4. In a ramekin add all of your ketchup, Mayonaise, Mustard, and a ppinch of Salt
  5. Using a spoon mix the sauces together until it looks like 1 coherent sauce
  6. Using a salad spinner or a colander, wash your lettuce to remove any earthy flavour and then set aside 2 lettuce leafs.
  7. Cut your burger bun in half horizonitally so that you have the top and bottom seciton of a burger
  8. Use half of your butter for each slice of bun to toast the bun in a pan
  9. Once the buns are toasted, heat some fat in the same pan and go get the beef mixture from the fridge
  10. Place the ball of mince straight onto the hot pan and immediatly smash with a burger smasher or spatula
  11. Flip when the burger is cooked halfway
  12. While the second half of the burger is cooking, place 2 slices of cheddar cheese on top of the burger to let it melt
  13. While our burger cooks, get your 2 burger bun slices and spread the sauce we made earlier onto each of them
  14. When the burger is finished cooking, assemble the burger: bottom bun, lettuce, burger (with cheese), lettuce, top bun
